# Expert Database for AI Boardroom

This is a comprehensive database of potential advisors across multiple domains. During boardroom setup, the skill uses this database to recommend 6-8 experts matched to the user's specific goals, skills, and expertise areas.

---

## HOW THIS DATABASE IS USED

When a user provides:
1. Their current abilities/skills
2. What they want to accomplish
3. Their 3-5 expertise areas

The skill searches this database to recommend experts whose proven methodologies and track records align with the user's specific context and goals.
