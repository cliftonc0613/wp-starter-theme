# Will's LinkedIn Content Grid Framework

## Overview

The LinkedIn Content Grid is a framework of **8 buckets** based on two dimensions:
- **Vertical axis (Theme)**: Do → Believe → Hate → Hope
- **Horizontal axis (Delivery)**: Teach vs. Tell a Story

This creates 24 proven prompt templates that eliminate writer's block and ensure well-rounded thought leadership.

---

## DO: Tangible Work
*Theme: Competence. Show your expertise and the specific work you perform.*

### Bucket 1: Teach What You Do
**Focus:** The "Secret Sauce," micro-skills, and technical processes.

**Prompt 1.1: The "How-To" Micro-Skill**
- **Topic:** Teach a very specific, small skill relevant to your job
- **Hook:** "Here are the 3 exact steps to [achieve result] without [pain point]."
- **Outline:**
  - Context: Why most people get this wrong
  - Step 1: The preparation
  - Step 2: The execution (the technical part)
  - Step 3: The refinement
  - Takeaway: Try this today and see [specific benefit]

**Prompt 1.2: The 60-Second Lesson**
- **Topic:** Simplify a complex concept
- **Hook:** "Most people overcomplicate [Concept]. Here is how to understand it in 60 seconds."
- **Outline:**
  - Define the concept simply (no jargon)
  - Give a real-world analogy (e.g., "Think of cash flow like a water tank...")
  - Explain why this matters for your reader's bottom line

**Prompt 1.3: The Tool/Stack Reveal**
- **Topic:** The specific tools or software you use to get results
- **Hook:** "I usually don't share my internal stack, but this tool saved me [Number] hours this week."
- **Outline:**
  - The problem: I used to waste time doing X
  - The solution: I started using [Tool Name]
  - The feature: My favorite feature is Y
  - The result: Now I can focus on Z instead

---

### Bucket 2: Tell a Story About What You Do
**Focus:** Bringing the work to life (First, Last, Best, Worst, Weirdest).

**Prompt 2.1: The "Mistake" Story**
- **Topic:** A time you messed up and what you learned
- **Hook:** "I used to think [Wrong Assumption]. Then I lost [Project/Money/Time]."
- **Outline:**
  - The Setup: I was confident about X
  - The Incident: Here is exactly what went wrong
  - The Lesson: Here is what I learned and how I changed my process
  - The Advice: Don't make my mistake

**Prompt 2.2: The "Weirdest" Request**
- **Topic:** A unique or surprising client project
- **Hook:** "I once had a client ask if we could [Strange Request]. I wasn't sure, but said yes."
- **Outline:**
  - The Challenge: Why this request was difficult/unusual
  - The Process: How we tackled it creatively
  - The Result: It actually worked better than expected
  - The Insight: Sometimes the weirdest ideas are the best

**Prompt 2.3: The "First Time"**
- **Topic:** Your first major win or project
- **Hook:** "I'll never forget the first time I successfully [Completed Major Task]. I was terrified."
- **Outline:**
  - The Emotion: Describe the imposter syndrome/fear
  - The Action: The one thing I focused on to get through it
  - The Outcome: It wasn't perfect, but it launched my career
  - Encouragement: If you're starting out, just take the first step

## BELIEVE: Your Philosophy
*Theme: Character. Your worldview and the "Why" behind your work.*

### Bucket 3: Teach What You Believe
**Focus:** Frameworks, manifestos, and core tenets.

**Prompt 3.1: The Manifesto / Principles**
- **Topic:** Your core operating principles
- **Hook:** "In the [Your Industry] industry, we focus too much on X. We need to focus on Y."
- **Outline:**
  - The Status Quo: What everyone else is doing
  - My Philosophy: The 3 pillars I believe in (e.g., Empathy, Speed, Honesty)
  - Why it works: Clients trust us because of this, not just our prices

**Prompt 3.2: The Contrarian Take**
- **Topic:** Disagreeing with a popular trend
- **Hook:** "Unpopular opinion: [Popular Strategy] is actually destroying your [Metric]."
- **Outline:**
  - The Trend: Everyone says you should "Move fast and break things"
  - The Reality: This actually leads to technical debt and burnout
  - The Better Way: Here is why we prioritize [Alternative]

**Prompt 3.3: The Definition**
- **Topic:** Redefining a common term in your niche
- **Hook:** "People often confuse [Term A] with [Term B]. The difference is costing you money."
- **Outline:**
  - Definition A: What people think it means
  - Definition B: What it actually means in practice
  - Application: How to apply the correct definition to your business today

---

### Bucket 4: Tell a Story About What You Believe
**Focus:** Origin stories and "Aha!" moments.

**Prompt 4.1: The Origin Moment**
- **Topic:** The specific event that formed your belief
- **Hook:** "I didn't always believe in [Core Value]. But then [Event] happened."
- **Outline:**
  - The Before: I used to operate like everyone else
  - The Event: Describe the moment/tragedy/win that shifted your perspective
  - The After: How I run my business differently now because of that day

**Prompt 4.2: The Mentor**
- **Topic:** Advice you received that stuck with you
- **Hook:** "Early in my career, a mentor told me: '[Quote]'."
- **Outline:**
  - The Context: I was young and confused about X
  - The Advice: They gave me this simple piece of wisdom
  - The Application: Here is how I used that advice in a meeting this week

**Prompt 4.3: The Hard Choice**
- **Topic:** Choosing values over profit
- **Hook:** "I once had to choose between a [High Dollar Amount] contract and my integrity."
- **Outline:**
  - The Offer: It was a dream deal on paper
  - The Catch: It required compromising on [Belief]
  - The Decision: I said no. Here is why
  - The Payoff: It hurt short-term, but attracted the right clients long-term

## HATE: Your Passion
*Theme: Injustice. Fighting the "Common Enemy" and protecting your clients.*

### Bucket 5: Teach What You Hate
**Focus:** Exposing traps, lies, and bad advice.

**Prompt 5.1: The "Trap" Warning**
- **Topic:** A bad product or strategy people buy
- **Hook:** "Stop buying [Product/Service]. It is the worst investment for 99% of people."
- **Outline:**
  - The Allure: Why people buy it (it sounds good)
  - The Math/Logic: Break down exactly why it fails
  - The Alternative: Here is where you should put your resources instead

**Prompt 5.2: The Industry Lie**
- **Topic:** Misinformation spread by competitors or "gurus"
- **Hook:** "There is a myth in my industry that says [Myth]. I am here to debunk it."
- **Outline:**
  - The Myth: "You need to post 10x a day to grow"
  - The Truth: Quality beats quantity every time
  - Evidence: Here is what we see with our actual data

**Prompt 5.3: The "Fake Guru" Callout**
- **Topic:** Bad advice you saw recently
- **Hook:** "I saw an 'expert' advise people to [Bad Tactic] yesterday. Please don't do this."
- **Outline:**
  - The Context: Saw a post/video claiming X
  - The Danger: If you do X, you risk [Negative Outcome]
  - The Fix: Do Y instead. It takes longer but lasts forever

---

### Bucket 6: Tell a Story About What You Hate
**Focus:** The client rescue mission.

**Prompt 6.1: The Rescue Story**
- **Topic:** Fixing a mess created by someone else
- **Hook:** "A client came to me in tears after being burned by [Competitor/Bad Service]."
- **Outline:**
  - The Situation: They had spent thousands and got zero results
  - The Villain: The previous agency didn't care about [Value]
  - The Hero (You): We came in and fixed the foundation
  - The Result: They are finally seeing growth

**Prompt 6.2: The Turning Point**
- **Topic:** Why you left your old job/industry
- **Hook:** "I quit my corporate job when I saw my boss do [Unethical Thing]."
- **Outline:**
  - The Environment: I was working at a "prestigious" firm
  - The Breaking Point: I realized they cared more about quotas than people
  - The Shift: That's why I started my own firm—to do it the right way

**Prompt 6.3: The "Enough is Enough"**
- **Topic:** A rant about a current annoyance
- **Hook:** "Does anyone else hate when [Software/Process] does [Annoying Thing]?"
- **Outline:**
  - The Rant: It drives me crazy because it wastes so much time
  - The Connection: I know I'm not alone in this
  - The Solution: Here is the workaround I found

## HOPE: Their Transformation
*Theme: The Future. Selling the destination, not the plane ride.*

### Bucket 7: Teach What You Hope
**Focus:** Vision, roadmaps, and trends.

**Prompt 7.1: The Visionary Post**
- **Topic:** What the perfect future looks like for your client
- **Hook:** "Imagine if your business didn't have to deal with [Pain Point] ever again."
- **Outline:**
  - The Vision: Picture a workflow where leads come in automatically
  - The Reality: This isn't sci-fi. It's possible now
  - The Map: Here are the 3 changes you need to make to get there

**Prompt 7.2: The "What If"**
- **Topic:** Challenging limits
- **Hook:** "What if you could achieve [Result] without [Sacrifice]?"
- **Outline:**
  - The Assumption: Everyone thinks you have to work 80 hours to scale
  - The Shift: What if you focused on leverage instead?
  - The Example: Here is what happens when you prioritize rest

**Prompt 7.3: The Optimistic Forecast**
- **Topic:** A positive trend in your industry
- **Hook:** "I am incredibly bullish on the future of [Industry]. Here is why."
- **Outline:**
  - The Observation: I'm seeing more companies prioritize [Positive Trend]
  - The Impact: This is going to lead to better products and happier teams
  - The CTA: Are you ready to ride this wave?

---

### Bucket 8: Tell a Story About What You Hope
**Focus:** Case studies and testimonials.

**Prompt 8.1: The Case Study (Before/After)**
- **Topic:** A specific client win
- **Hook:** "Meet [Client Name]. From [Negative State] to [Positive State] in 90 days."
- **Outline:**
  - The Problem: They were stuck at a revenue plateau
  - The Diagnosis: We found a bottleneck in their sales process
  - The Fix: We implemented [System]
  - The Stats: Revenue is up 40%

**Prompt 8.2: The "Proud Dad" Moment**
- **Topic:** Celebrating a client's personal success
- **Hook:** "I just want to shout out my client [Name]. They just hit a massive milestone."
- **Outline:**
  - The Grind: They have been working quietly on this for months
  - The Win: They finally launched [Project]
  - The Feeling: It's an honor to play a small part in their journey

**Prompt 8.3: The Personal Goal**
- **Topic:** Achieving something you hoped for
- **Hook:** "Last year, I wrote down a goal to [Goal]. It felt impossible."
- **Outline:**
  - The Doubt: I almost didn't write it down because I was scared
  - The Work: It wasn't a straight line. I failed twice
  - The Arrival: Yesterday, we crossed the finish line
  - The Lesson: Hope requires action

## Quick Reference: Choosing Your Bucket

**When your idea involves...**
- **Teaching a skill or process** → Bucket 1 (Do) or Bucket 3 (Believe) or Bucket 5 (Hate) or Bucket 7 (Hope)
- **Telling a personal story** → Bucket 2 (Do) or Bucket 4 (Believe) or Bucket 6 (Hate) or Bucket 8 (Hope)
- **Work or expertise** → Do buckets (1-2)
- **Your values or philosophy** → Believe buckets (3-4)
- **Industry injustice or passion** → Hate buckets (5-6)
- **Vision or transformation** → Hope buckets (7-8)
