## TEACH PHASE - KEY MESSAGES (Read These Aloud)

### Message 1: "AI Is Just Patterns"
"AI = machines learning patterns from data. Not magic. Not conspiracy. Patterns."

### Message 2: "You Can Do This"
"You don't need to be a programmer. You need curiosity. That's it."

### Message 3: "Three Types (Easy Version)"
- **Predictive:** Guesses what comes next (Netflix)
- **Generative:** Creates new things (ChatGPT)
- **Analytical:** Finds insights in data

### Message 4: "Real People. Real Jobs."
Tell 3 stories (Marcus, Sarah, Jamal). They all started where students are now.

## BUILD PHASE - THE FIVE PROMPTS

### Prompt 1: Customer Email (0:55-1:17)
**Problem:** Customer angry, need response fast
**Result:** Draft empathetic apology
**Why it matters:** Saves time, improves customer service

### Prompt 2: Meeting Summary (1:17-1:39)
**Problem:** Long meeting, need notes fast
**Result:** Summary + action items
**Why it matters:** Increases productivity

### Prompt 3: Data Analysis (1:39-2:01)
**Problem:** Have data, want insights
**Result:** AI finds patterns in data
**Why it matters:** Makes data actionable

### Prompt 4: Content Creation (2:01-2:23)
**Problem:** Need social content, fast
**Result:** 3 engaging social posts
**Why it matters:** Content creation at scale

### Prompt 5: Problem-Solving (2:23-2:45)
**Problem:** Stuck on retail crowding issue
**Result:** 5 practical low-cost ideas
**Why it matters:** Strategic thinking

## INSTRUCTOR MOVES (What to Do During BUILD)

**Every 10 minutes:**
- [ ] Walk around room
- [ ] Ask: "How's it going?"
- [ ] Look for "yellow flags" (stuck, frustrated)
- [ ] High-five completions

**For Stuck Students:**
- "What's the problem you're trying to solve?"
- "What would you want AI to help with?"
- "Copy the prompt exactly, then press enter"

**For Advanced Students:**
- "Can you make the prompt more specific?"
- "What if you asked it a follow-up question?"

**For Whole Group (If Many Struggle Same Thing):**

- Then continue.

## SHOWCASE STRATEGY (Last 15 Min)

**Goal:** Celebrate, validate, build community

**If volunteers don't raise hands:**
"I'll go first. Here's my best customer email response. What do you notice?"

**For each volunteer:**
- Let them talk (1 min)
- You ask: "What made this work?" (20 sec)
- Peers respond: "That's clever..." (20 sec)

**Your closing:**
"You learned what AI is. You built 5 real prompts. You made a portfolio piece. That's advanced work. Well done."

## VIBE CHECK (Read This Right Before Class Starts)

**You are:**
- Enthusiastic (AI is exciting, show it)
- Encouraging (they can do this)
- Real (not corporate, not fake)
- Available (not lecturing from podium, walking around)
- Patient (first time for many)

**You are NOT:**
- Trying to be the smartest person
- Impatient with questions
- Talking down to them
- Making it harder than it is

**Remember:** You're not teaching AI experts. You're building confidence in 3 hours. That's your real job.
